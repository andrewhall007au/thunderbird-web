## Notes

1. **Permit Requirements**: Many popular trails now require permits (Angels Landing, Half Dome, The Wave, Enchantments). Check current regulations before planning.

2. **Trail Conditions**: GPX files may not reflect current trail conditions, closures, or reroutes. Always check with land management agencies.

3. **Accuracy**: User-uploaded GPX files vary in quality. Official sources (PCTA, CDTC, NPS) are most reliable.

4. **Offline Use**: Download GPX files before heading to trailheads, as cell service is often unavailable.

5. **Data Currency**: Long-distance trail data (PCT, AT, CDT) is updated periodically. Check for the most recent versions.
